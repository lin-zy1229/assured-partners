Differentiation
“In most cases differentiation is not what you have, it is how you do it. Better process leads to better results” – Randy Schwantz Author of The Wedge
Be Unique
Your competition follows a simple, tired and typically ineffective formula.
  • Tell my company’s story
  • Gather relevant information from the prospect
  • Market the account
  • Pray for a good price
    • Following this formula is inefficient and will almost always lead to a false sense of security for the agent. You have typically won an account on either price or coverage and the result is that you will likely lose the account the same way.
    Setting the Stage
    Setting the stage so the prospect comprehends that his/her expectations for your role in his/her business SHOULD be different, will help him/her understand that you are different and there to make a difference in the performance of their insurance package.
    Your job is to proactively control the experience of your clients, making their future predictable
