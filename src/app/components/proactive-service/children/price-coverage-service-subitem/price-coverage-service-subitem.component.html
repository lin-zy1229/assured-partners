Price, Coverage & Service
Price
Your competition loves to talk about price
  • They have markets!
  • They have programs!
  • They will go find a better price!
    • But is this truly a “competitive advantage”? No. Price is a fickle mistress. If you sell on price you will eventually lose on price.
    Coverage
  • Selling on coverage could be likened to selling a car because it has an engine
    • Finding areas where a prior agent has missed a coverage is a great door opener, but it will not keep a client for the long term.
    Service
  • Almost all of your competition is good at one thing, nearly without fail: “REACTIVE” service. Agents who lack a good track record in reactive service aren’t agents for long
  • “PROACTIVE” service is another story
