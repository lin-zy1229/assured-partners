Rules of Prospect Engagement
1. Put yourself in your prospect’s shoes
2. Speak English
3. Know your processes
4. Be ready to walk away
5. Give the prospect a reason to take you seriously
6. Don’t talk about yourself or your company – this is about him or her
Randy Schwantz and The Five Did Theys . . .
When Randy Schwantz wrote “The Wedge” he determined five questions that needed a yes answer before the prospect was qualified. They are:
  • Did they tell you what the competition wasn’t/isn’t doing?
  • Did they tell you that was a problem?
  • Did they tell you what they wanted?
  • Did they invite you to come back?
  • Did they tell you they could fire their current agent?
