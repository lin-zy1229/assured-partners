So . . . how do you distinguish yourself from your competition, without throwing out the same old tired lines?

The first thing to understand is that you MUST allow your buyers to reach their own conclusions. Boxing people into a corner by “overcoming objections” does not and never has stimulated mutually satisfying relationships.

For Discussion

  • What are some good questions you can ask your prospect to relax them and stimulate conversation?

  • Would you use analogies to communicate your points?

  • If so, what are some good ones you can think of now?

  • How can you illustrate that life with your firm will be better without just coming out and stating it?
