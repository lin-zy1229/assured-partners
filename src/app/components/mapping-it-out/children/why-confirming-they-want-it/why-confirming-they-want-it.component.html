Why? - Confirming They Want it

This is it, you’ve communicated what it is you do and how you and your team do it. Now we need to know one thing – does your prospect want it?

This step is simple, the verbiage is simple and their response should be easy to read. You are going to say:

Why?-Confirming They Want it
You are going to say
Roll Back the Tape

You're going to say

You
“Okay, I think I’ve got it. I only have one more question. (Pause like you are pondering how to phrase it) Why is doing it this way important to you Mr. X? (Pause) Or better yet, what do you see as the consequences if you DON’T do it this way?”

Wait. Watch their body language, most often, this is a great test to see if they’ve been listening and are seriously contemplating your services.

If their response is something close to your catastrophic scenario during the “Devil’s Advocate” part, then you probably have a legitimate prospect.

You are looking for something like this:

You
“Well, if I’m hearing you right, if we fail to have this Claims Review done each year, we could and actually probably would end up paying more premium and not even know about it! That could add up really quick!”

---BOOM!---

Roll Back the Tape

Once you’ve got your confirmation that this is something they want and you are sure they understand the consequences of neglect, then say:

You
“Let me be sure I’m on the right track and I’m hearing what you want, correctly. Okay with you?”

Simply start with
You
“You want a claims review, you want it done the first week of July . . . “
When you finish ask:
You
“Did I miss anything, or is that exactly how you want this done?”

You

“Are you ready to get back to that Building Valuation now?”
