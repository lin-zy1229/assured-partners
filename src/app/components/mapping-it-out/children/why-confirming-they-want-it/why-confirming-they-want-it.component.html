Why? - Confirming They Want it

This is it, you’ve communicated what it is you do and how you and your team do it. Now we need to know one thing – does your prospect want it?

This step is simple, the verbiage is simple and their response should be easy to read. You are going to say:

Why?-Confirming They Want it
You are going to say
Roll Back the Tape

They will often say:

“I’m not sure, you brought
it up?”

It will be your tendency, and human nature by the way, to simply start puking on them about when YOU think it should be done –

You should respond instead with:

“I know a company a lot like yours,
and they decided to do it
the first week of July because they wanted . . .”

Fight off the urge to talk about yourself or your experience, focusing instead on the prospect, their experience and what is better for them! More on this during “How”

Collaboration and the Buyer’s Perception We are trying to change the buyer’s perception of what this experience has probably always been like, and begin to subtly communicate that we are collaborating! This is the beginning of your Written Service Timeline for next year!
How refreshing it will be to our prospect to learn you are not there to tell them what to do, but instead to hear what they want!
