The Basics

1. Stop talking about yourself and your company 2. Communicate your strengths through the use of analogies, instead of preaching about your achievements 3. Ask the buyers if they are still comfortable terminating their current relationship 4. What you have developed is your property 5. If they can’t or won’t play by your rules, you leave

Technique: Setting Price Aside

Problem: If you hand out a binder, know that nothing you say for the next minute or so is going to register. Your buyer will instantly begin combing through the content until they find the page with the rates.
Solution: Bring a single sheet of paper and have the rate printed on it and hand that out before you say anything about your proposal. Then ask them, “Okay, is it alright to start digging into what is behind that number?”
