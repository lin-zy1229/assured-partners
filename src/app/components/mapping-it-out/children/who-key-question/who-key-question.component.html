Who? - Key Question (Jerry - CFO)
Okay, what is Jerry's last name?
How long has Jerry been your CFO?
How did he get into the role, was he promoted, did he come from another company?
Can you walk me through his typical day? How does he get his hands dirty?
Would it be fair to call Jerry your right hand man?
Why did you mention him? Would he be a person who would be getting updates on the status of the process?
Repeat this process for any people your prospect brings up. If they stall or are having problems coming up with anyone ask for the usual suspects – those whom you know deal with this stuff, HR people, Financial people, their assistants, etc.
Remember to ask them if they see their broker playing a role in the service – what is it? How much do they expect from him/her?
