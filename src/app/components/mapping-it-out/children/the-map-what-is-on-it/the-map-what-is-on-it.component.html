What? What – is the Service Called
Who? Who at “ABC” Manufacturing would be involved?
When? When (what time of year) would you want this to happen?
How? How is it going to work (what is the process)?
Why? Why is doing it important?
What is it– You will begin with something like: “So, in regards to this Claims Review . . . I’m going to walk you through a series of questions to be sure I thoroughly understand what you want and how you want it done. It is important to me that I understand all the different aspects of why you want it, because every company is different . . . Does that make sense?”
Help the Prospect Understand What is Coming Next The prospect has probably never been through an exercise like this. Most likely, their experience has been that they are told what is going to happen and how it is going to be done – so let’s coach them a little to make the process effective
