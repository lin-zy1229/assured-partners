Search Spotlights
1. Job Changes: Spotlight identifies prospects who have changed jobs within the last three months
2. Shared Experiences: Spotlight uncovers prospects who attended the same schools, worked at the same companies, or belong to the same LinkedIn Groups as you
3. LinkedIn Activity: Spotlight shows prospects who have posted or shared content on LinkedIn in the past 30 days
4. Mentioned in the News: Spotlight finds prospects who have been mentioned in the news in the past 30 days
5. Brand Engagement: Spotlight identifies prospects who have fol lowed your company or commented on your company’s posts on LinkedIn
Saved Searches
Searching in Sales Navigator also provides the unique ability to save your frequent searches
  • You can easily access recent and saved searches from the homepage
  • You can access the list of your saved searches from the Search bar dropdown
  • When you save your searches, you will receive automated alerts when new leads match your criteria
  • Those new leads will be delivered straight to your inbox when they are identified
