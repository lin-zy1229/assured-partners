Touches - Week 4 - Organizing Your Contacts
Week Four
Set Meeting? Categorize as “A”
Potential? Categorize as “B”
No Activity? Categorize as “C”
Week 4 -
  • Categorize all the leads from these campaigns as “A”, those who have engaged and with whom you’ve set a meeting
  • “B” potential “A” leads that have liked a post, responded to an email or Inmail, etc. but haven’t agreed to a meeting
  • “C” those from whom you haven’t seen any activity
  • File your “B” and “C” contacts to use again later in another campaign
