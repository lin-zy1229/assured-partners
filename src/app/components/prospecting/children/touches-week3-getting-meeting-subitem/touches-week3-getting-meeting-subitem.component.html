Week 3 -
  • If you have had email responses, click-throughs on postings, likes on postings, and/or comments on postings – note those showing interest and begin to establish a “Top Contacts” list
  • Begin to utilize your “Inmail” allotment to reach out to those you have categorized as “Top Contacts” opportunities – you get 30 per month, use them judiciously and wisely – if you get a response, that Inmail does not count towards your 30 (see the sidebar at the right for Inmail direction)
  • Email the contacts who have not made your “Top Contacts” list with the same content as used in your Inmails
  • Follow up your Inmails and Emails with phone calls and face to face cold calls – see page 13.
