Touches - Week 1 & 2 - Beginning Your Relationship Campaign
Cold Calling Averages
Some statistics:
  • 2% of sales are made on the first contact
  • 3% on the second contact
  • 5% on the third contact
  • 10% on the fourth contact
  • 80% of sales are made between the fifth and twelfth contact
  • 44% of sales reps quit after “No. 1”
    • Using only the phone to attempt to reach a prospect means on average, you must make eight attempts per prospect just to get a conversation. Your odds of setting a meeting after that? One in 35.
    When to Call?
    Time?
  • 4:00 to 5:00 is best
  • 8:00 to 10:00 is next
  • 11:00 to 2:00 is worst
    • Day of the Week?
  • Thursday is best
  • Wednesday is next
  • Tuesday is the worst day to call
    • Whom to Call?
  • Firm with 100-500 employees has an average of seven people in the buying process – reach out to multiple contacts
  • CEOs & CFOs are obvious – dig deeply into the HR department, risk management, etc.

    Week 1 -
  • Invite all the contacts on the lead lists to connect
  • Mail a hand written card to each of the contacts stating that you would like to meet them and learn more about their business
  • Four or five days later send them an Email asking only if they received the card – that’s all
