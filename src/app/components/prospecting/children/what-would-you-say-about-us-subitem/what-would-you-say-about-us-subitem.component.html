What Would You Say About Us?

If you leave it up to your source to come up with a dialogue, the odds are pretty good they will not have a positive effect. What is the person you want to meet going to think?

  • Why is my friend doing this to me?
  • I already have a guy/gal
  • Oh, wait, this sounds interesting, perhaps there is something in it for me?

    • Obviously we are looking for the third possible outcome. How do we get there? We coach our source, subtly, to encourage the outcome we desire.
