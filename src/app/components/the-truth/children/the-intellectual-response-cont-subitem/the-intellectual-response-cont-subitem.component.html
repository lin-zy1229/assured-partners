The Intellectual Response Cont.

The point is, asking a question like that one, or even a direct question like “If I come through with what you want, can you fire your guy/gal?”, is likely to produce a thoughtless response or at least one that isn’t thoroughly considered. The prospect isn’t necessarily lying, but they probably aren’t taking your question seriously.

That is why it is necessary to follow a process which will put them in a position to consider the topic and to understand you need a seriously considered response before moving on to the next step.
