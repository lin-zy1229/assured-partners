The Intellectual Response

“If I am able to do the things we’ve discussed today, would you be willing to do business with me (us)?”

Have you said it or some version of it?

How did your prospect respond? Did they say yes? Of course they did.

That is called an intellectual response. Some might call it a “knee jerk” reaction, or even just telling you what you want to hear because it is easier than even a minor confrontation.
