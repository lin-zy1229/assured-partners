Setting the Stage Cont.

The scenario would likely come after you have mapped out a few proactive services and collaborated with the prospect on how those services would play out in their business. I call it “Mapping it Out”. In Blueprint it may come after the Discovery session. If you do not have a process you currently employ, it would follow a point where you see yourself ready to ask for materials so you can work your magic and develop a proposal.

Regardless of what you do, skipping the question of whether or not they are willing to fire their incumbent will certainly lead you to a lower closing ratio.
