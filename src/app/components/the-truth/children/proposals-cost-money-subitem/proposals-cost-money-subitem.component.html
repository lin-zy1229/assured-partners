Proposals Cost Money

It may not have occurred to you, but each time you submit a prospect to the marketing department or ask the staff to prepare a capabilities presentation, it cost a significant amount of money.

How much depends on the circumstances, but the average amount is about $4,000.

So the question is – if I asked you to write me a check for four grand when you offer a submission to the marketing team, are you confident enough in your chances of success to do that, knowing I’m going to cash the check if you fail?
