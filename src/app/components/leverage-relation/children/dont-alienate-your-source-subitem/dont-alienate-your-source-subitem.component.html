Don’t Alienate Your Source

The odds are good that your source will not have made the call for the introduction the first time you follow up.

  • Remind your source this is a voluntary activity and ask them “Are you still comfortable with this?”
  • Set another follow up date – if they still haven’t made the call, set the next follow up date for at least a week down the line or simply ask your source when they’d like to set up the meeting.
    • Call Back Questions

    Once they've set the appointment

  • What’s going on with his/her company?
  • What did you tell him/her? How did they respond?
  • Did you talk about his/her current agent and their relationship?
  • Did he/she bring up any specific questions or needs regarding his/her company?
