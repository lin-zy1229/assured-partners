A Referral? Or an Introduction?

Why Make a Distinction?

Isn’t a referral and an introduction essentially the same thing? No, not when it comes to creating a relationship.

A referral is best probably best illustrated when you consider a visit to your doctor. Doctors have specialties. If you are visiting your general practitioner because you have a rash, he or she may very well “refer” you to a dermatologist for treatment. Why? Simple. Because you NEED a dermatologist.

An Introduction is Better

If you take the time to explain to your source what they are getting into and why, your chances for success increase dramatically. Make them part of the process, encourage their input and finally, coach them on how to present the meeting to the person you want to meet so they understand how they might benefit.
